Roadmap

  • Phase 0 — Research: Validator interviews, builder outreach, grant prep.
  • Phase 1 — Registry & DAO: Validator registry MVP, multisig treasury, governance parameters.
  • Phase 2 — Telemetry & Pilot: Watchers live, epoch scoring, pilot payouts (10–20 validators).
  • Phase 3 — DeFi & Audits: Bond tokenisation (MPT/TEA), AMMs, streaming payouts, security review.
